Hariyali Social Empowerment Foundation is a continuation of Hariyali Samsarika Samiti, contributing to social welfare since 2011.

Our Key achievements:

  • ✔ 1,000 school kits delivered, fueling young minds with the tools to succeed.
  • ✔ 111 women empowered with two-wheelers, unlocking new paths to independence.
  • ✔ Countless lives uplifted through skill development and economic support.

Our mission is to foster education and economic empowerment, especially for underprivileged communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals through education, skill development, and economic assistance, fostering equal opportunities and creating a sustainable, inclusive society where everyone can reach their full potential.

Our Vision

Our vision at Hariyali Social Empowerment Foundation is to create a society where everyone has opportunities to thrive through access to education and economic independence, fostering better communities.
